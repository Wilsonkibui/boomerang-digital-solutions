Warranty & Returns Policy

Warranty Coverage

All our laptops and desktops come with a standardized warranty period (e.g., 6 months for refurbished, 1 year for new) unless otherwise stated. This warranty covers hardware defects that occur under normal use.

What is Covered:

  • Hard drive or SSD failures
  • RAM defects
  • Motherboard issues not caused by liquid damage or power surges
  • Screen defects (dead pixels, lines) present upon arrival

What is NOT Covered:

  • Physical damage (cracks, dents, drops)
  • Liquid damage
  • Software issues (viruses, OS corruption)
  • Battery life degradation over time (consumable)

Returns & Exchanges

If you receive a defective item, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery. items must be returned in their original condition with all accessories.
